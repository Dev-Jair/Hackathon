About Us

Mission

Kami Online is a company made up of gamers. We excel because gamers rely on teamwork, are wicked problem-solvers and tenacious about reaching their goals, whether it's beating a raid boss or launching a product. Our mission is to keep on evolving and staying ahead of the curve by designing innovative top-of-the-line products and services-allowing gamers to fully experience their entertainment.

Vision

While evolving our procuts, we also want to evolve the whole company. By working on faster support resolutions, easy access to new learning systems and a great feedback system, we want to keep on investing in our staff. We understand that the backbone of the company is built by our employees. By investing even more time and energy in our own people, we can offer our customers a great gaming experience!

What we stand for

Gameplay first

Everything we do at Kami Online is based on the succes of the gaming experiences we provide our players. The goal of each discipline within the company -- be it art, programming or customer support -- is to make our controllers as fun as possible for as many people as we can reach.

Commit to Quality

We approach each task carefully and seriously. We seek honest feedback and use it to improve the quality of our work. At the end of the day, most players just want to enjoy their games and have fun each and everyday!

Learn & Grow

The games industry is ever-changing. Technology improves, techniques change, and design philosophies become outdated. Since the founding of Kami Online, we've worked to improve through experience, teaching one another and cultivating the desire to be the best at what we do. We see this as an individual responsibility as well as a company one. Employees can count on their peers, managers, and the company itself to be supportive and help them gain the knowledge and training they need.
